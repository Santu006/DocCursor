COMPLAINT FOR BREACH OF CONTRACT AND ACCOUNT STATED

Plaintiff ABC Manufacturing Corp. ("Plaintiff"), by and through its attorneys, for its Complaint against Defendant RiverNorth HVAC Services, Inc. ("Defendant"), states as follows:

PARTIES AND JURISDICTION

1. Plaintiff is a Delaware corporation with its principal place of business in Chicago, Cook County, Illinois.

2. Defendant is an Illinois corporation with its principal place of business in Evanston, Cook County, Illinois.

3. This Court has jurisdiction under 735 ILCS 5/2-209 because Defendant transacts business in Illinois and the claims arise from a contract performed in Cook County. Venue is proper in Cook County.

FACTS

4. On or about June 8, 2022, Plaintiff and Defendant entered into a written Purchase Agreement (the "Contract") under which Defendant agreed to purchase HVAC control modules from Plaintiff for installation on commercial projects.

5. Between July 2022 and April 2023, Plaintiff delivered Goods invoiced at Three Hundred Eighty-Four Thousand Two Hundred Dollars ($384,200), as reflected on Invoice Nos. ABC-7721 through ABC-7798.

6. Defendant accepted the Goods without timely written rejection.

7. Despite repeated written demands dated May 2, 2023, and July 14, 2023, Defendant has failed to pay the outstanding balance of Two Hundred Sixteen Thousand Five Hundred Dollars ($216,500).

8. The Contract provides that overdue amounts accrue interest at one and one-half percent (1.5%) per month and that the prevailing party may recover reasonable attorneys' fees.

COUNT I — BREACH OF CONTRACT

9. Plaintiff realleges paragraphs 1–8.

10. Defendant breached the Contract by failing to pay amounts due.

11. As a direct result, Plaintiff has been damaged in the amount of at least $216,500, plus interest and attorneys' fees.

COUNT II — ACCOUNT STATED

12. Plaintiff realleges paragraphs 1–8.

13. Defendant received and retained account statements reflecting the balance due and did not object within a reasonable time, constituting an account stated.

PRAYER FOR RELIEF

WHEREFORE, Plaintiff respectfully requests that this Court enter judgment in favor of Plaintiff and against Defendant for:

(a) Compensatory damages of $216,500;
(b) Prejudgment interest as provided in the Contract and by law;
(c) Reasonable attorneys' fees and costs; and
(d) Such other relief as the Court deems just and proper.
