MINUTES OF THE MEETING OF THE BOARD OF DIRECTORS

Date: March 12, 2024
Time: 10:00 a.m. Central Time
Place: 100 Industrial Parkway, Chicago, IL 60601 (Board Room) and via secure video conference

Directors Present:
- David Chen, Chair
- Priya Shah
- Marcus Lee
- Olivia Grant
- Robert Kim (joined at 10:08 a.m.)

Also Present:
- Helen Park, Corporate Secretary
- Anita Rao, outside counsel (Shah & Associates LLP), for Item 4 only

1. CALL TO ORDER

The Chair called the meeting to order at 10:02 a.m. A quorum was present.

2. APPROVAL OF PRIOR MINUTES

Upon motion duly made and seconded, the Board approved the minutes of the January 9, 2024 meeting unanimously.

3. DISTRIBUTION AGREEMENT — MIDWEST REGIONAL DISTRIBUTORS LLC

Ms. Shah presented the proposed Exclusive Distributor Agreement with Midwest Regional Distributors LLC for Illinois, Indiana, and Wisconsin, including a $250,000 standby letter of credit and five-year term.

RESOLVED, that the Board hereby approves the Exclusive Distributor Agreement substantially in the form presented, and authorizes Priya Shah, VP Sales, to execute the Agreement and related documents on behalf of the Corporation.

The resolution passed 5–0.

4. LITIGATION UPDATE — RIVERNORTH HVAC SERVICES, INC.

Ms. Rao reported on Case No. 2023-L-008742 pending in Cook County Circuit Court. Outstanding principal claimed remains $216,500. Settlement discussions are ongoing in the range of $175,000 to $190,000, subject to Board approval of any final settlement above $150,000.

RESOLVED, that management and counsel are authorized to negotiate settlement of Case No. 2023-L-008742 for an amount not to exceed One Hundred Ninety Thousand Dollars ($190,000), inclusive of interest waived, with final documentation subject to Chair approval.

The resolution passed 5–0.

5. NOVATECH NDA AND OEM EXPLORATION

The Board noted execution of the Mutual NDA with NovaTech Controls Inc. dated January 15, 2024, and authorized continued technical diligence under that NDA, without committing the Corporation to an OEM agreement.

6. ADJOURNMENT

There being no further business, the meeting adjourned at 11:15 a.m.

Respectfully submitted,

/s/ Helen Park
